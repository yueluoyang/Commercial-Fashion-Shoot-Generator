# Indoor Set Styles

Recommend 2-3 indoor set styles after garment analysis. Each option must be compatible with the garment's color, silhouette, fabric, and commercial sales purpose.

## Option Format

```text
Option A - <style name>
Mood: <season, emotion, visual language>
Set: <room architecture, furniture, floor/wall>
Props: <2-4 restrained props>
Light: <direction and quality>
Why it fits: <specific link to garment color/fabric/silhouette>
Risk control: <what must stay visible>
```

## Style Library

Use or adapt these, keeping the choices specific to the product:

### Spring Sunlit Cream Studio

- Mood: fresh, airy, soft spring, slight breeze.
- Set: cream walls, sheer curtains, light oak floor, subtle wall molding.
- Props: white tulips, pale greenery, linen stool or chaise, ceramic vase.
- Light: warm window sunlight with soft shadows.
- Best for: pale colors, soft dresses, feminine ruffles, romantic commercial images.

### Modern Gallery Apartment

- Mood: polished, quiet luxury, clean editorial commerce.
- Set: off-white gallery wall, travertine side table, oak or stone floor, low sculptural chair.
- Props: single branch arrangement, neutral ceramic, folded linen.
- Light: broad diffused side light.
- Best for: structured dresses, minimal tailoring, column silhouettes, premium understated pieces.

### Garden Room Conservatory

- Mood: indoor spring garden, natural, refined, breezy.
- Set: bright indoor room with tall windows, pale stone or wood floor, soft green accents.
- Props: potted olive tree, fresh flowers, woven chair, linen curtain.
- Light: sun filtered through glass and sheer fabric.
- Best for: green, floral, chiffon, lace, fluid, or nature-toned garments.

### Soft Parisian Salon

- Mood: elegant, feminine, graceful.
- Set: ivory panel molding, herringbone floor, vintage mirror, small upholstered stool.
- Props: pale flowers, pearl-toned ceramic tray, sheer drape.
- Light: warm late-morning window light.
- Best for: dresses with drape, ruffles, refined evening or occasionwear.

### Minimal Warm Loft

- Mood: contemporary, clean, calm.
- Set: warm plaster wall, light wood floor, large window, simple linen chair.
- Props: sculptural vase, low plant, neutral rug.
- Light: soft directional sunlight.
- Best for: simple silhouettes, knitwear, neutral products, everyday elevated pieces.

## Recommendation Rules

- Do not recommend outdoor scenes unless the user overrides the workflow.
- Avoid busy sets that compete with the garment.
- Do not let props cover the front, back, hem, waistline, or key construction details.
- For pale garments, use slightly warmer or darker neutral backgrounds so the product edge remains readable.
- For saturated garments, use restrained cream, stone, wood, or muted green surroundings.
- For romantic garments, prefer curtains, flowers, and soft furniture.
- For sharply tailored garments, prefer gallery, loft, or architectural settings.
